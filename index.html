Who We Are

wholesome smoothies, a subsidiary of EAAB Confiance Ltd, is a brand new provider of freshly blended assorted fruits drink of health and vitality based in Ogun state, Civic Center Building, Federal Polytechnic Ilaro.
Hygenically we utilize natural organic raw materials and blend assorted fruits of extremely high nutritional benefits, void of preservatives, for healthy human consumption and eating lifestyle.
The core of our business is to formulate and combine correctly hygenic nutritional assorted fruits (smoothies), rich in fibre and supplementary contents for overall wellness and good health.
Our range of products includes; Smoothies, Tigernut Milk, Date, Coconut Milk, Garlic and Ginger mix, fruit salad, Coles Law and more. We offer tailored-suit services at affordable price to our clientele based which includes; Office/home delivery, corporate meetings/conference, Birthday parties, wedding ceremonies and more. Also we are wholly committed and steadfast to providing high-end quality products and services using state-of-art equipment/blenders with 100% environmental friendly workstation thus efficiently and effectively meeting customers demand.
